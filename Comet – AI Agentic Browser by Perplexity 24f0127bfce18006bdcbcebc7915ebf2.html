Comet – AI Agentic Browser by Perplexity

1. Executive Summary

Perplexity recently launched Comet, an AI-powered, agentic web browser built on Chromium and designed to transform browsing into seamless task-oriented workflows. Available initially to Perplexity Max subscribers ($200/month), Comet aims to replace tab management with conversational task automation while preserving user privacy.

This case study outlines a PM-led strategy to enhance Comet's usability, adoption, and expansion through improved workflows, accessibility, and user trust.

2. Market & Business Context

  • Browser Market: Google Chrome commands ~68% global share (Reuters, The Verge).
  • AI Trends: Browsing is shifting from passive navigation to conversational intelligence (Perplexity AI, Quartz).
  • Perplexity’s Position: Newly valued at ~$20B, with investors like Nvidia and SoftBank. Recent bold $34.5B bid for Chrome illustrates its ambition (Business Insider, Android Central, Financial Times).
  • Challenges: Early critiques focus on the invite-only rollout and performance limitations (InfoQ, Tom's Guide).

AI Browser Trend Analysis

  • AI adoption in productivity tools: 64% of knowledge workers now use AI weekly (Gartner, 2025).
  • Shift from Search → Task Execution: Tools like Comet, Arc, and Opera AI are moving toward intent-based browsing (you say the goal, AI executes it).
  • Rise of Agentic Workflows: AI acts as a “web co-pilot” — booking, summarizing, and acting across sites.
  • Consumer Trust Issues: Privacy and data usage controversies remain top barriers.

3. Problem Definition

User Need: Users (PMs, researchers, professionals) want to:

  • Move beyond search & dozens of tabs
  • Perform tasks like booking, summarizing, comparing—without context switching

    Challenges:

  • AI workflow complexity vs. latency and subscription cost
  • Onboarding limitations (invite-only)
  • Privacy concerns with content usage (Reuters, Business Standard)

4. User Personas

Persona 1 – Knowledge Worker

  • Name: Sarah, 32, works remotely
  • Goal: Run multi-step tasks (research, email, booking) quickly and context-aware
  • Pain Point: Too many open tabs, disconnected tools, wasted time during task execution

Persona 2 – Productivity Enthusiast

  • Name: Rahul, 28, freelance consultant
  • Goal: Automate repetitive browsing tasks (flight booking, summary creation)
  • Pain Point: Frequent copying across tabs, lost browsing flow, fear of misuse

5. Opportunities (Feature Ideas)

  1. Guided Onboarding Tour – familiarizing users with agentic features.
  1. Task Templates – prebuilt workflows like “Plan a trip” or “Product research.”
  1. Local Performance Optimization – reduce latency on AI tasks.
  1. Publishing Partnerships – Resolving Content Usage Concerns.
  1. Broader Access Tiers – lower-cost plans or tiered onboarding.

7. Go-To-Market Strategy

Pre-launch:

  • Invite influencers and productivity reviewers for early feedback
  • Release tutorials and demo videos

Launch:

  • Promote task templates through ads and content marketing
  • Community challenge: “What can Comet do in 10 minutes?”

Post-launch:

  • Add performance toggle (lite mode)
  • Expand the invite list with referral codes
